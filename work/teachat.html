  • ABOUT & CONTACT

TeaChat is a mobile app facilitating meetups between strangers. We found that with the right proding and hosting, meeting strangers can be a serendipitous event, because everyone has a story to tell. Every chat is unique, different, and memorable. Chats usually start with a group of strangers sitting around a table introducing formalities. Somewhere along the line, someone will share something that crosses the line of superficiality, inspiring everyone else to let their walls down. By the end of the conversation, you'll be wondering why we don't do this more often.

More will be coming soon about the process, but in the meantime, check out our website!
