CASESTACK - CONSOLIDATION

consolidating small orders into truckloads

Due to NDA, please contact me for a private link with more details! :)

One of the creative ways CaseStack employed to combat frequent struggles in logistics is consolidation, a well known strategy giving CaseStack its highest percent margins in recent years. Consolidation is grouping small orders from different customers into a huge truck. The ensured route from only Point A to Point B gives it a higher chance of arriving on time, lowers cost and resources significantly.

However, as more information is added, the more their excel sheets are prone to error, making it difficult to expand efficiently. How can we help the team manage more consolidation with less problems and less time involved?

Some design considerations based on research:

  • supporting collaboration to get things right the first time
  • flexibility and maintainability in settings, but beware of data corruption
  • providing visibility for errors for more data accuracy
  • supporting intuitive interaction necessary for making changes & avoiding information overload due to showing just the right amount for the context
  • results from beta testing
