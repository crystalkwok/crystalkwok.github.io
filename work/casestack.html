CASESTACK

improving products and experiences with logistics (and adding some fun!)

I am currently a product designer (focusing on UX) with CaseStack, a logistics solutions company. I am involved in all parts of the design, including researching with observations and interviews, wireframing with Balsamiq, designing with Photoshop and Illustrator, and prototyping with InvisionApp and HTML/CSS/JS. Personally, I'll be doing some inspired illustrations from logistics! Our team also serves as the product owner for CSIL, the new internal site we have been tasked to redesign. I'll be posting some of our accomplishments from our time here, as well as some of our wireframes, designs, and results when I get the chance.

DURATION: August 2015 - Present

ROLE: Product Designer (UX)

TOOLS: Contextual Inquiry, Ethnographic Studies, Adobe Creative Suite, Storyboarding, Wireframing, Prototyping

COLLABORATORS: Loren Stewart, Evangeline Lee, the 720 Team

Illustrations for Logistics

I've recently been inspired by Dropbox-- file syncing isn't a fun, cute thing. But their illustrations and designs are AMAZING, and they've truly been able to add a more fun and personable touch to their business without interfering with other people's business-- and in fact, making it a better experience. Logistics isn't fun-- but I've decided to be creative and make it more fun and personable with illustrations.
