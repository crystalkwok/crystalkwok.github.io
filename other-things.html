Dance Progression · Learn more about the concentration

This "concentration" includes pieces that an artist completes to deeply investigate a theme. I finished 8 pieces following a deep investigation of dancers, which I called "A Dancer's Journey." In this concentration, the backgrounds and dancers gradually transform from realistic to more fantasy-like. The dancer starts with a normal position, then gradually does more dancer-like positions and movements. The element of lines on the dancer is added to highlight the dancer's beauty that comes from the uniqueness and fluid lines they make with their body. The dancers gradually become more fantasy-like, have more movement, and have more lines throughout their bodies to show their journey in finding their identity as a dancer and transforming into a true dancer.
