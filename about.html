Hello there! I'm Crystal, formerly a Cognitive Psychology major, Computer Science minor student at the University of California, San Diego. I fell in love with human-centered / UX design because not only does it combine all my passions (people, research, design, and programming), but it has the power to solve problems for change through creative ideas based on user needs. Not only does design change through research to fit the different users, needs, and contexts in our ever changing world, but design also has the power to change how people think and engage with products and experiences. My education and research in Psychology helps me focus on users and understand the motivations that drive them. I am currently a Product Designer at CaseStack, a third-party logistics company, helping to design the experience and flow of people involved with logistics, and influence product vision. I am also contributing to the design efforts at the Cultivation Project, a new missions network for easier updates.

When I'm not brewing on how to improve designs and products, I am crafting, dancing, watching Masterchef Australia, obsessing over typography, and serving with my church. :)

Currently located in:       Los Angeles
