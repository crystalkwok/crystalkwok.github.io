Hi, I'm Crystal (again)

I'm currently a product designer at Stanley Black & Decker focusing on connecting materials to the field in construction technology. I studied Cognitive Psychology with a Computer Science minor at University of California, San Diego, where Don Norman was starting to bring up the design discipline. I fell in love with human-centered design because it not only combines all my passions (people, research, design, and programming), but it has the power to create solutions that people desire to use to improve and impact their lives. I’m always aiming to stay curious, ask questions, and design for impact.

When I’m not designing, I’m illustrating & managing a stationary shop for @animalfactdoodle, creating calligraphy pieces, learning Chinese, eating great LA SGV food, and dancing. (I guess there's actually more designing here than I thought )
